IMC-W Fire Alarm Panel

The IMC-W Fire Panel is a wireless panel desiged to operate on the 868MHz band and is also equipped with a wired loop in adition to the wireless. Its typical use is in Public area's of an appartment house or a when in a need for a small panel which can activate external Fire bells or calling the Fire Brigade. The small Built in Imc panel has 2 loops where one is connected to a IRF-W wireless signaltransmitter and the other loop is available for a local wired fire loop. The panel is equipped with a strong 120dB sirene.
